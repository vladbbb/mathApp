Pindalaühikud

ruutmillimeeter, ruutsentimeeter, ruutdetsimeeter, ruutmeeter, aar, hektar ja ruutkilomeeter on pindalaühikud.

1 ruutmillimeeter =1mm2 = 0.0000001m2
1 ruutsentimeeter = 1cm2 = 0.00001m2
1 ruutdetsimeeter = 1dm2 = 0.001m2
1 ruutmeeter = 1m2 = 100dm2=10000cm2=1000000mm2
1 aar =1a =100m2
1 hektar =1ha=10000m2
1 ruutkilomeeter =1km2 =1000000m2
